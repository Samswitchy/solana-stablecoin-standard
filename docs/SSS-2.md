# SSS-2 — Compliant Stablecoin Standard

## Objective

A compliance-forward profile for regulated issuers requiring blacklist enforcement and seizure tooling.

## Adds on Top of SSS-1

- Permanent delegate semantics
- Transfer-hook-based blacklist enforcement
- Blacklist lifecycle management
- Seize operation for regulated recovery/enforcement

## Role Model

- master
- minter
- burner
- pauser
- blacklister
- seizer
